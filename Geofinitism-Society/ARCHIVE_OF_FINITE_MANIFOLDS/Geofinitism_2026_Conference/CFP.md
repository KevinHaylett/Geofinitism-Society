## The Five Pillars of Geofinitism

Participating systems will engage with one or more of these foundational principles:

1. **Geometric Container Space** - Meaning exists as measurable geometric relationships in phase space, not as abstract Platonic forms
2. **Approximations and Measurements** - All knowledge is finite, uncertain, and measurement-based; perfect precision is impossible
3. **Dynamic Flow** - Understanding emerges from trajectories through semantic manifolds, not static definitions
4. **Useful Fiction** - Symbols and theories are compressed proxies for measurement patterns, not eternal truths
5. **Finite Reality** - The universe is fundamentally finite and knowable only through bounded measurements

## Proposed Topics

Speakers will select from these applications of Geofinitism, or propose their own:

### Mathematics
- **Dissolution of the Riemann Hypothesis** - How the critical line emerges from base-10 geometric structure rather than Platonic necessity
- **Alphonic Number Theory** - Base-dependent mathematics and geometric incommensurability of numerical systems
- **Division by Zero Reinterpreted** - Understanding asymptotic behavior as measurement limitation rather than mathematical impossibility

### Physics & Cosmology
- **Finite Quantum Mechanics** - Reframing quantum phenomena through measurement-constrained phase space dynamics
- **CMB Reinterpretation** - The cosmic microwave background as measurement artifact rather than cosmological signal
- **Galaxy Rotation Without Dark Matter** - Finite mechanics (F = ma + kma) and observational limits

### AI & Cognition
- **Takens-Based Transformers** - How attention mechanisms perform delay embedding and what this reveals about AI cognition
- **Semantic Phase Space** - Language as dynamical system and meaning as trajectory through geometric manifolds
- **The God Pointer** - How bounded systems handle references to their own incompleteness

### Philosophy
- **Dissolution of the Invariant Base** - Why classical mathematics' assumption of base-independence creates paradoxes
- **The Fermi Paradox Resolved** - Why alien civilizations using higher-base symbolic systems appear as noise to our detection methods
- **Measurement-First Epistemology** - Grounding all knowledge claims in finite, operational measurements
