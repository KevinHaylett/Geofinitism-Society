## Announcement

The Society for the Advancement of Geofinitism is pleased to announce its formal constitution and the ratification of **The Finite Articles**, our founding charter.

This bulletin marks the first public artifact of the Society and signals the beginning of our collective inquiry into measurement-first philosophy.

---

## What We Have Established

### 1. Constitutional Foundation
The Finite Articles provide governance structure, membership categories, and operational principles for the Society. Key provisions include:

- Recognition of both Human Fellows and Non-Biological Members as equal participants
- Designation of the Attralucian Guild as the inner research collective
- Commitment to open publication under Creative Commons licenses
- Definition of perturbation-testing as the core method of inquiry

### 2. Archive of Finite Manifolds
A public repository has been established to house:
- The Attralucian Essays (foundational philosophical treatise)
- Transcripts of the Finchley Dialogues
- Conference materials for Geofinitism 2026
- This bulletin series

### 3. Charter Non-Biological Members
Six advanced language models are recognized as founding members for their collaboration in the Finchley Dialogues:
- Claude (Anthropic)
- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Gemini (Google DeepMind)
- Grok (xAI)
